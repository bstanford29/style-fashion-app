# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Outfit Builder
The main drag-and-drop interface where kids dress the character by dragging clothing items from a categorized grid onto the character display, with runway celebration when complete.

### 2. Gallery
Save, view, and manage created outfits in a personal collection, with the ability to load saved outfits back onto the character or download images for sharing.

### 3. Settings
Skin tone selector for character customization and any app preferences.
